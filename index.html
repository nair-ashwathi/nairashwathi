About Me

Namaste! I am a PhD researcher at Swinburne University of Technology and a part of OzGrav in Melbourne, Australia. My research involves evolving massive binaries and studying the formation of massive binary neutron stars. Apart from astrophysics, you can find me independently studying neuroscience and behavioural psychology, or making mystic art.

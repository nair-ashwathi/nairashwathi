About Me

Namaste! I am a second-year PhD researcher at The Swinburne University of Technology and a part of The ARC Centre of Excellence for Gravitational Wave Discovery (OzGrav) in Melbourne, Australia. My full-time job involves evolving massive binaries and studying the formation of massive binary neutron stars and doing life. Apart from astrophysics, you can find me independently studying neuroscience/neurobiology and behavioral/child psychology OR making mystic art.

Research Interests: Stellar Astrophysics, Binary evolution (all masses), Gravitational waves
